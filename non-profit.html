The Bay Area is our home and where we live and work. To show our appreciation and support to our local community, every quarter we select a local non-profit organization to donate our in-kind gift of on-site chair massage.

Giving Back

Who can receive this gift?

Any local non-profit organization with 501 c (3) status. Organizations can apply directly or be nominated by one of our clients.

How does it work?

Apply prior to the due date using the form below (Non-profit organizations can apply directly OR be nominated by a client of Synergy Massage & Wellness)

Winners selected by our team of therapists. Free massages are delivered in January, April, July and October
