Synergy Massage & Wellness is committed to fostering balance, wellness and productivity in workplaces throughout the Bay Area. Through our quality-focused, customizable mobile onsite chair massage programs, we provide employers an affordable way to increase the well-being and satisfaction of their most important asset: their employees.

Rates

Our mobile onsite chair massage rate is $90 per hour, with a one time setup fee of $25 per therapist. This price includes gratuity and our booking minimum is 3 hours. Non-profit qualify for a 15% discount.

Cancellation Policy

Quotes of $1,000 or more require a 50% deposit. For quotes less than $1,000 we require a $100 deposit. You can cancel your appoitment up to 72 hours in advance. With less than 72 hours notice, 50% of the total booked amount will be assessed directly or deducted from any deposit. Your deposit can be applied to reschedule service in the future.
